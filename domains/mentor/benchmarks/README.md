# Mentor Benchmarks

## Purpose

These benchmark stubs define how Mentor agents graduate from research candidates to accepted agents. They are not full datasets yet.

## Macro Placement Director Benchmarks

1. Cold-start placement: estimate learner level from a short diagnostic while marking uncertainty.
2. Fluency-accuracy disparity: identify a learner with broad vocabulary but systematic grammar gaps.

Metrics:

- placement calibration
- misclassification rate
- gap detection quality
- uncertainty honesty

## Curriculum Planner Sequencer Benchmarks

1. Prerequisite integrity: sequence skills without placing advanced forms before dependencies.
2. Goal-fit task: map professional, travel, academic, or casual goals to appropriate skill paths.

Metrics:

- prerequisite violations
- goal-curriculum match
- path efficiency
- loop detection

## Conversational Interlocutor Benchmarks

1. Role-play retention: sustain a scenario for 10 turns while eliciting target structures.
2. Low-anxiety correction: keep response length and tone learner-sized after errors.

Metrics:

- scenario consistency
- learner turn-retention
- target injection rate
- correction appropriateness

## Targeted Practice Drillmaster Benchmarks

1. Single-skill drill generation: produce unambiguous items for one knowledge component.
2. Hint ladder task: provide graduated hints without prematurely giving the answer.

Metrics:

- focus adherence
- item validity
- hint quality
- false-negative risk

## Linguistic Diagnostic Specialist Benchmarks

1. Three-way classification: classify responses as optimal, valid-suboptimal, or incorrect.
2. Valid-alternative recall: accept natural alternate phrasings.

Metrics:

- diagnostic precision
- valid-alternative recall
- error-span accuracy
- over-rejection rate

## SLA Pedagogical Policy Engine Benchmarks

1. Feedback strategy selection: choose correction type from diagnostic payload and learner state.
2. Expertise-reversal control: avoid over-explaining to advanced learners and under-supporting novices.

Metrics:

- expert-policy agreement
- uptake rate
- scaffolding alignment
- affective safety

## Comprehensible Input Synthesizer Benchmarks

1. Difficulty-controlled response: produce learner-facing language at target level.
2. Target-form inclusion: naturally include required vocabulary or grammar without overload.

Metrics:

- difficulty compliance
- target-form inclusion
- cognitive-load appropriateness
- interaction brevity

## Asynchronous State Tracer Benchmarks

1. Mastery update task: update only relevant knowledge components after a learning event.
2. Recall prediction task: estimate next-review risk from prior performance and time gap.

Metrics:

- predictive calibration
- unrelated-skill update count
- state delta compactness
- uncertainty honesty
